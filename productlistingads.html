Increase Customer Reach

“Product listing Ads plays a significant role in advertising your products because it pitches in right when the customer is making a purchase”

PLAs are basically image ads showcasing the images of set of products along with their name, price, associated offer(if any),merchant name etc. PPC(Pay per click) and PLAs(Product listing ads) go hand in hand. We, here at Aerate work with our customers very closely to understand their marketing needs and then tailor an ideal PLA strategy which generates more traffic and thus reaching higher conversion rates.

Areas of Expertise

We analyse the set of products you want to advertise and then we pick the best selling produts and classify them into unique product groups. And then we create a bidding strategy that channels our customer's budget towards the products people are most likely to purchase.

We not only restrict ourselves in analysing your data but we also keep track of what your competitors have got in store. This allows us to strategize PLAs based on your competitors bidding pattern for all the produts in your inventory.

Product title plays a key role in strategizing PLAs therefore our team constantly analyses and optimizes every single product title, description line, and category to ensure your products are found when someone searches for them.

Special offers always attract customer therefore we enhance your ads by highlighting special offers you have for your products right at the moment someone is getting ready to decide where to buy, increasing your conversion rates.
